Step 3: Run Simulation

Once you successfully completed Step 1: Create a Case and Step 2: Create a Mesh, you are ready to run your simulation on DesignSafe.

  1. In the tab, select the folder containing the case
  2. Notice that the buttons on the bottom, in particular is now available. Click this button to go to the parameters screen.
  3. On the left side of the screen are tabs for 'mesh' and 'sim'. For the 2D type of case, first a simulation mesh must be created. Then the simulation can actually be run. There are parameters for each process.
  4. Under the simulation step, there are parameters for simulation duration, timestep, turbulence modeling and more.
  5. At any time, you can click 'save all parameters' to save your changes. Click 'run' to begin running the simulation. Under the 'remote jobs' tab, you will be able to view the list of agave jobs in progress and monitor the eventual completion of your simulation.
