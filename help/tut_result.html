Step 4: View Results and Download

Once your case has finished running, you can view results.

  1. Select the folder containing the case in the tab.
  2. Notice that the buttons on the bottom, in particular is now available. Click this button to go to the results screen.
  3. This screen will show a list of available results, depending on the type of case you have run. For each result file [1], either a view [2] or a download [3] action icon or both will be available as applicable, allowing you to view or download that result by clicking on the respective action icon.

    Furthermore, at the bottom the window [4], you may download the entire set of results by clicking
